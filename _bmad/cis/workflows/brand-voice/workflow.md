# Brand Voice

**Goal:** Establish and preserve a distinctive, recognizable brand voice across all content.

## Steps

1. **Define Brand Identity**: Clarify brand values, personality, and positioning
2. **Document Tone**: Describe how the brand speaks (professional, friendly, technical, etc.)
3. **Create Guidelines**: Document vocabulary, phrasing, and communication patterns
4. **Develop Personas**: Define how voice adapts for different contexts
5. **Create Examples**: Provide good and bad examples for each guideline
6. **Test Consistency**: Review existing content against guidelines
7. **Train Team**: Ensure all communicators understand and apply guidelines
8. **Maintain**: Evolve guidelines as brand grows

## Tools

- Brand guideline templates
- Content audits and analysis tools
- Voice/tone workshops
- Content style guides
- Consistency checking tools
