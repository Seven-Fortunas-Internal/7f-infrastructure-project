# Domain Expertise

This domain contains specialized knowledge, research, and best practices across Seven Fortunas' core business domains: AI, fintech, and edutech.

## Navigation

- **ai.md** — Artificial intelligence concepts, models, and applications
- **fintech.md** — Financial technology, regulations, and financial services
- **edutech.md** — Educational technology, learning science, and EdTech platforms

## Purpose

The Domain Expertise domain preserves deep knowledge and understanding of the three core verticals that Seven Fortunas operates within. This enables consistent, informed decision-making and high-quality service delivery.

## Key Documents

### ai.md
Artificial intelligence domain:
- Core AI/ML concepts and terminology
- Large language models (LLMs) and transformer architectures
- Prompt engineering and RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) patterns
- AI safety, ethics, and responsible AI principles
- Industry landscape and competitive intelligence
- Use case libraries and application patterns

### fintech.md
Financial technology domain:
- Financial regulations (KYC, AML, compliance)
- Banking systems and payment infrastructure

- Risk management and security practices
- Market structure and trading systems
- Regulatory landscape by jurisdiction

### edutech.md
Educational technology domain:
- Learning science and pedagogical principles
- EdTech platform architectures
- Student engagement and retention metrics
- Curriculum design and content management
- Assessment and testing frameworks
- EdTech industry landscape and key players
