# Culture

This domain contains organizational values, mission statements, and team structure documentation for Seven Fortunas.

## Navigation

- **values.md** — Core values, principles, and organizational ethos
- **mission.md** — Mission statement, vision, and long-term goals
- **team.md** — Team structure, roles, responsibilities, and contact information

## Purpose

The Culture domain preserves and communicates the organizational ethos, values, and structure that guide decision-making and team alignment at Seven Fortunas.

## Key Documents

### values.md
Core organizational values:
- Fundamental principles guiding decisions
- Ethical commitments and standards
- Cultural pillars and behavioral expectations
- Values in action: examples and case studies

### mission.md
Organizational mission and vision:
- Mission statement (purpose and impact)
- Vision (long-term aspirational goals)
- Strategic objectives and key results
- Market positioning and competitive advantage

### team.md
Team structure and responsibilities:
- Organizational chart and reporting lines
- Role descriptions and responsibilities
- Team member directory with contact info
- Communication protocols and decision-making frameworks
- Meeting cadences and synchronization points
