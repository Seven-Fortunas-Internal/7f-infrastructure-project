# Team Structure

## Organizational Overview

Seven Fortunas operates as a distributed, autonomous team organized around core functions: Product & Engineering, Customer Success, and Operations.

## Leadership Team

### Executive Leadership
- **CEO / Founder**: Overall strategy, fundraising, external relationships
- **CTO / VP Engineering**: Technical architecture, engineering culture, roadmap
- **VP Product**: Product strategy, roadmap, customer insights
- **VP Operations**: Finance, legal, HR, business operations

## Engineering Team

### Core Responsibilities
- Design and implement AI-native infrastructure
- Maintain platform reliability and performance
- Develop integrations and SDK/API tooling
- Write and maintain documentation
- Respond to production incidents

### Specializations
- **Backend/Infrastructure**: Core platform, APIs, data systems
- **Frontend**: Dashboards, UIs, developer experience
- **DevOps/SRE**: Deployment, monitoring, reliability
- **Security**: Threat modeling, compliance, security practices
- **Data Engineering**: Analytics, data pipelines, insights

### Team Norms
- Primary communication: Async-first with daily standups
- Code review required for all merges to main
- On-call rotation for production support
- Weekly engineering sync for planning and architecture

## Product & Design

### Responsibilities
- User research and validation
- Feature design and specification
- Roadmap prioritization
- Customer feedback integration
- Competitive analysis

### Workflow
- Bi-weekly product planning
- Design review before implementation
- User testing for significant features
- Product metrics and analytics

## Customer Success

### Responsibilities
- Onboarding new customers
- Support and issue resolution
- Training and documentation
- Customer feedback collection
- Renewal and expansion relationships

### Support Channels
- Email: support@example.com
- Slack: #support channel
- GitHub Issues: For bug reports and feature requests
- Office hours: Weekly scheduled support sessions

## Operations & Finance

### Responsibilities
- Financial planning and reporting
- Legal and compliance
- People operations (hiring, culture)
- Business development
- Vendor management

### Policies
- Travel approval thresholds documented separately
- Procurement process documented in operations runbook
- Hiring approval process through leadership team

## Communication Structure

### Synchronous Meetings
| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|----------|---------|
| Engineering Standup | Daily | 15 min | Status updates, blockers |
| Product Sync | Weekly | 30 min | Roadmap, prioritization |
| Engineering Sync | Weekly | 1 hour | Architecture, planning |
| Leadership Sync | Weekly | 1 hour | Cross-functional alignment |
| All Hands | Monthly | 1 hour | Company updates, culture |

### Asynchronous Communication
- **GitHub Issues**: Feature requests, bugs, decisions
- **Slack channels**: General, engineering, support, random
- **Email**: Important announcements, formal communications
- **Wiki/Docs**: Knowledge base and reference materials

### Decision-Making Framework
1. **Decisions by Data**: Measurable outcomes drive choices
2. **Decisions by Expertise**: Subject matter experts have input weight
3. **Decisions by Consensus**: Build alignment before major changes
4. **Escalation Path**: Unclear decisions escalate to relevant leader
5. **Transparency**: Decisions and reasoning documented

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Individual Contributor
- Deliver high-quality work within area of expertise
- Contribute to code review and knowledge sharing
- Participate in on-call rotation if applicable
- Provide feedback in retrospectives
- Help other team members succeed

### Tech Lead / Senior IC
- Design systems and technical direction
- Mentor junior engineers
- Lead design reviews
- Contribute to architectural decisions
- Own critical systems or features

### Manager
- Support and develop team members
- Conduct 1:1s and provide feedback
- Hire and onboard new team members
- Facilitate team meetings and culture
- Escalate blockers and removal of obstacles

### Director / Leader
- Set team vision and strategy
- Define OKRs and metrics
- Allocate resources and prioritize
- Build and maintain team culture
- Represent team to broader organization
