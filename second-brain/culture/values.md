# Core Values

## Our Fundamental Principles

Seven Fortunas is built on values that guide our decision-making, shape our culture, and define how we work together.

### 1. **Excellence Through AI**
We embrace AI as a foundational tool for scaling quality, not replacing human judgment. We pursue technical excellence while maintaining human oversight and ethical grounding.

**In Practice:**
- Build AI-native systems thoughtfully, not just for hype
- Prioritize reliability and safety over speed
- Invest in testing and validation
- Document why we chose each approach

### 2. **Radical Transparency**
We operate openly, share decisions, and invite challenge. We believe trust is built through honest communication and visibility.

**In Practice:**
- Share decisions and reasoning, not just outcomes
- Acknowledge limitations and failures
- Make data and plans accessible to the team
- Encourage respectful dissent

### 3. **Autonomy with Accountability**
We empower individuals to make decisions while holding ourselves and each other accountable for results.

**In Practice:**
- Give context, then trust judgment
- Expect others to own their work and decisions
- Create feedback loops that build capability
- Celebrate learning from failures

### 4. **Inclusive Expertise**
Expertise comes in many forms. We value diverse backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives in building better solutions.

**In Practice:**
- Actively seek different viewpoints
- Create space for quiet voices
- Recognize expertise wherever it exists
- Mentor and develop talent continuously

### 5. **User-Centered Thinking**
We build for real problems, not imagined ones. We stay connected to our users' needs and validate our assumptions.

**In Practice:**
- Talk to users regularly
- Measure impact, not just effort
- Iterate based on feedback
- Keep solutions simple until complexity is necessary

### 6. **Sustainable Pace**
We believe in doing our best work over a sustainable period. Burnout doesn't produce excellence.

**In Practice:**
- Respect work-life balance
- Set realistic deadlines
- Celebrate progress, not just completion
- Rotate high-stress work across the team

## Behavioral Expectations

### What We Value
- **Curiosity**: Asking "why?" and learning continuously
- **Collaboration**: Sharing knowledge and lifting others up
- **Ownership**: Taking initiative and following through
- **Integrity**: Doing what we say; being honest about challenges
- **Pragmatism**: Getting things done despite uncertainty

### What We Challenge
- Blame-shifting or avoiding accountability
- Gatekeeping knowledge or information
- Ignoring user feedback or data
- Pursuing perfection at the expense of progress
- Isolated decision-making without team input

## Living Our Values

### For Managers
- Model the values you expect
- Hire for values alignment
- Give feedback that reinforces our principles
- Remove barriers to autonomous work
- Celebrate value-aligned decisions, even if they don't succeed

### For All Team Members
- Speak up when decisions misalign with values
- Help teammates live out these principles
- Share successes and failures openly
- Ask for help and support others
- Question approaches, not people
