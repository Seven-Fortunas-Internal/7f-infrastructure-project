# Operations

This domain contains operational runbooks, incident escalation procedures, and secrets management protocols for Seven Fortunas infrastructure.

## Navigation

- **runbooks/** — Standard operating procedures and incident response guides
- **escalation/** — Escalation procedures and on-call rotation
- **secrets-management/** — Credentials, keys, and secret management protocols

## Purpose

The Operations domain enables reliable, consistent infrastructure management and incident response. It documents procedures for maintaining system health, responding to issues, and managing sensitive credentials securely.

## Key Documents

### Runbooks
Standard operating procedures:
- Service startup and shutdown procedures
- Deployment and release processes
- Database maintenance and backups
- Monitoring and alerting setup
- Health checks and diagnostics
- Common troubleshooting guides
- Incident triage and initial response

### Escalation
Incident management and escalation:
- On-call rotation and responsibilities
- Escalation paths by issue severity
- SLA targets and incident response times
- War room procedures and communication
- Incident post-mortems and retrospectives
- Status page updates and stakeholder communication
- Key contact information and team pages

### Secrets Management
Secure credential handling:
- Secret storage locations (vaults, KMS, etc.)
- Credential rotation schedules
- Access control and permissions
- Audit logging for secret access
- Secret injection in CI/CD pipelines
- Emergency credential revocation procedures
- Secret lifecycle management
